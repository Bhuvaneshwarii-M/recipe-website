Ingredients
Thing Quantity
Tuvaram Paruppu 1 cup
Urad dal 1/2 cup
Bengal gram dal 1 cup
Red Chilli 3 cup
Pepper 1/2 cup
Coriander 2 cup
Fenugreek 1/2tsp
Turmeric Powder 1/4tsp
Cumin 1/2 cup
Asafoetida Powder 2
Curry Leaves 8




Recipe


  • Roast all the ingredients separately and grind them to powder.
