Creative - POTATO BALLS

"A recipe has no soul. YOU as the cook must bring SOUL to the recipe."


Ingredients
Thing Quantity
Potatoes 300g
Coriander leaves 3tsp
Black pepper 1tsp
Salt required amount
Cornflour 1/2cup
Oil 2tbsp
Black Cardamom 1(coarsely crushed)




Recipe


  • Boil the potatoes and peel it.

  • Smash it in a bowl and add coriander leaves, black pepper, salt, cornflour, oil and mix it well.

  • Apply oil and make small balls with hand.

  • Fry the balls on low flame.

  • Take the balls out of oil when they turn golden brown colour.
