Creative - CHICKEN DONUT

"A recipe has no soul. YOU as the cook must bring SOUL to the recipe."


Ingredients
Thing Quantity
Boneless Chicken 450g
Coriander leaves 1/2 cup
Mint 1/2 cup
Green Chillies 3
Onion Powder 1tbsp
Garlic Powder 1tbsp
Chilli Powder 1tsp
Turmeric Powder 1/2tsp
Pepper 1tsp
Salt required amount
Cheese 1 cup
Breadcrumbs 1/2 cup
Eggs 2




Recipe


  • Blend boneless chicken, coriander, mint, green chilli, onion powder, garlic powder, chilli powder, turmeric powder, pepper and salt into a paste.

  • Add cheese and breadcrumbs to the mixture and mix it well.

  • Grease your hand with oil and mould it into a donut shape and refrigerate it for 15 mins.

  • Whisk eggs with salt and pepper in a plate.

  • Coat the chicken donut with egg mixture and breadcrumbs.

  • Fry it on medium heat both sides until golden and crispy.
